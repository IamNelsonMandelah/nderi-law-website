Ann Nderi

LLB & LLM, University of Nairobi
Advocate of the High Court of Kenya
Member, Law Society of Kenya
Governance & Policy Practitioner

Ann Nderi is an Advocate of the High Court of Kenya and Managing Partner of Nderi Law & Associates Advocates. With over 15 years of experience guiding clients through complex legal and regulatory compliance matters, Ann brings a rare combination of technical excellence and human-centred advocacy.

Respected for her strategic legal counsel and dedication to client-focused solutions, Ann is also a governance and policy specialist — consultant for GIZ, World Bank, USAID, and Equality Now — and an IVLP Alumnus of the U.S. Department of State.

What We Do

Areas of Practice

Comprehensive legal services delivered with precision, discretion, and a client-first approach.

Corporate & Commercial Law

Business structuring, company secretarial services, commercial contracts, mergers & acquisitions, compliance, and regulatory advisory for businesses of all sizes.

Employment & Labour Relations

Employment contracts, workplace dispute resolution, disciplinary processes, unfair termination claims, and advisory on Employment Act compliance.

Land & Property Transactions

Conveyancing, title searches, sale agreements, land transfers, lease agreements, and resolution of land disputes before the Environment & Land Court.

Contract Drafting & Review

Careful drafting and review of commercial agreements, service contracts, partnership deeds, and NDAs to protect your interests and minimise risk.

Family Law

Matrimonial matters, divorce proceedings, child custody & maintenance, adoption, and domestic violence protection orders — handled with empathy and discretion.

Probate & Succession Planning

Will drafting, estate administration, grant of letters of administration, succession certificates, and dispute resolution for inheritance matters.
